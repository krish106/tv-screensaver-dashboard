# Privacy Policy for Glance

**Last Updated: December 1, 2025**

This Privacy Policy describes how Glance ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Android TV application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Wallpaper Images**: Photos and videos you upload to use as screensavers are stored locally on your device.
- **Manual Location**: If you choose to manually enter a city name for weather information.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **IP-Based Location**: When using automatic weather detection, your IP address is sent to ipapi.co to determine your approximate location (city/region).
- **Device Information**: Basic device information for app functionality and crash reporting.
- **Weather Data**: Temperature, humidity, and weather conditions fetched from OpenWeatherMap API based on your location.

### 1.3 Information Collected by Third Parties

#### Google Play Billing

- When you purchase a subscription through Google Play Billing, Google processes your payment information.
- We do not have access to your payment card details.
- Google Play's privacy policy applies: <https://policies.google.com/privacy>

#### Google AdMob (for free users)

- AdMob may collect advertising identifiers, device information, and usage data to serve personalized ads.
- You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.
- Google Ads privacy policy: <https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads>

#### OpenWeatherMap API

- Your location (coordinates or city name) is sent to OpenWeatherMap to retrieve weather data.
- OpenWeatherMap privacy policy: <https://openweathermap.org/privacy-policy>

#### ipapi.co (IP Geolocation)

- Your IP address is sent to ipapi.co for automatic location detection.
- ipapi.co privacy policy: <https://ipapi.co/privacy>

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

- **Displaying Screensavers**: Show your uploaded wallpapers and videos on your TV screen.
- **Weather Display**: Fetch and display current weather conditions for your location.
- **Subscription Management**: Manage your premium subscription status to remove ads.
- **Advertising**: Display ads to free users (removed for premium subscribers).
- **App Functionality**: Ensure the app works correctly on your Android TV device.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- **Wallpaper files** are stored in your device's external storage directory (`/storage/emulated/0/TV_Screensaver/`).
- **Settings and preferences** are stored locally using Android SharedPreferences.
- **Weather cache** is stored locally to enable offline access.

### Data Security

- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption.
- We do not transmit your wallpaper images to any external servers.
- Location data is only used for weather services and is not stored on external servers by us.

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

### Data Access and Deletion

- **Wallpapers**: You can delete your uploaded wallpapers at any time through the app or by navigating to `/storage/emulated/0/TV_Screensaver/` on your device.
- **Weather Settings**: You can disable weather features in the app settings.
- **Location Data**: You can switch between automatic and manual location, or disable weather entirely.
- **App Data**: Uninstalling the app will remove all settings and cached data.

### Advertising Preferences

- **Opt-Out of Personalized Ads**: Go to your device Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.
- **Ad-Free Experience**: Subscribe to Premium to completely remove ads.

### Account Deletion

- This app does not create user accounts.
- All data is stored locally on your device.
- To remove all data, uninstall the application.
