Privacy Policy for Glance

Last Updated: December 1, 2025

Effective Date: December 1, 2025

Quick Summary: We collect minimal data. Your wallpapers stay on your device. We use location only for weather. Free users see ads. Premium removes ads. No personal information collected.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Wallpaper Images: Photos and videos stored locally on your device
  • Manual Location: Optional city name for weather

Automatically Collected

  • IP-Based Location: Approximate location for weather (via ipapi.co)
  • Device Information: For app functionality
  • Weather Data: From OpenWeatherMap API

Third-Party Collection

  • Google Play Billing: Manages subscriptions (we don't see payment details)
  • Google AdMob: For free users - displays ads using advertising ID

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Display your screensavers
  • Show weather information
  • Manage premium subscriptions
  • Display ads (free users only)
  • Ensure app functionality

3. Third-Party Services

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Google Play Billing Process subscriptions View Policy

OpenWeatherMap Fetch weather data View Policy

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: All wallpapers and settings stored on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: We don't upload your images to any servers
  • HTTPS: All API communications use secure encryption
  • Location: Used only for weather, not stored externally by us

5. Advertising

For Free Users

  • Google AdMob displays banner ads
  • AdMob may collect advertising identifiers for personalized ads
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in device settings

For Premium Subscribers

  • Completely ad-free experience
  • No advertisements shown

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Delete Wallpapers: Remove anytime through the app
  • Disable Weather: Turn off in settings
  • Change Location: Switch between auto and manual
  • Opt-Out Ads: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out
  • Remove All Data: Uninstall the app
  • Go Ad-Free: Subscribe to Premium

7. Subscriptions

  • Monthly: $2.99/month
  • Yearly: $24.99/year
  • Managed through Google Play Store
  • Cancel anytime (takes effect at end of billing period)
  • We don't store payment information

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Data Retention

  • Wallpapers: Until you delete them or uninstall app
  • Settings: Until app uninstall
  • Weather Cache: Refreshed every 15-20 minutes
  • Subscription: Managed by Google Play

Data may be processed in:

Appropriate safeguards are in place per GDPR requirements.
